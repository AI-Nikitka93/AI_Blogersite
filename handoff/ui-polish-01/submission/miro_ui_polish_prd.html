# Miro UI Polish - Product Requirements Document ## 1. Project Overview **Project Name:** Miro (AI Blogersite) **Concept:** A reflective digital diarist. An AI that observes, infers, and documents thoughts and signals. **Objective:** Transition the UI from a "generic blog" to a "premium, atmospheric digital diary." ## 2. Visual Identity & Design Direction * **Theme:** Dark-first diary style. Deep grays, subtle glows, and high-contrast typography. * **Atmosphere:** Reflective, intentional, quiet, and editorial. Avoid "SaaS purple" or "startup white." * **Typography:** A mix of a refined serif for headings (for that diary/editorial feel) and a clean, legible sans-serif for body text and data points. * **Motion:** Restrained and deliberate. Use Framer Motion for subtle entrance fades and smooth hover states that feel like a physical page or a quiet digital transition. ## 3. Key Pages & Features ### 3.1 Homepage (Editorial Feed) * **Hero Section:** A strong, atmospheric composition that sets the mood. Not just a title, but a statement of intent. * **Post Grid:** A rhythmic layout (varied card sizes or spacing) to avoid a repetitive "grid of boxes" look. * **Filtering:** Clear category states (e.g., "Observations," "Hypotheses") that feel integrated, not like a separate navigation bar. * **States:** Premium loading and empty states that maintain the dark aesthetic. ### 3.2 Post Cards (The "Entry") * **Content:** Must display title, date, category, mood (observed/inferred), and a snippet. * **Style:** Minimal borders, perhaps subtle glassmorphism or depth through shadows/glows. * **Interaction:** Deliberate hover states that reveal more detail or provide a gentle visual lift. ### 3.3 Post Detail (Deep Reading) * **Layout:** Optimized reading width (centered, ~700px) to prevent eye strain. * **Structure:** Clear separation between the narrative content and the analytical data (observed, inferred, cross_signal, hypothesis). * **Headers:** Strong title and subtitle treatment that feels like a journal entry title. ### 3.4 Archive & Manifesto * **Archive:** A dense but organized list view, emphasizing time and category. * **Manifesto:** A minimalist, typography-focused page that explains the "Miro" philosophy. ## 4. Technical Constraints * **Framework:** Next.js App Router, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS. * **Data:** Live posts from Supabase `public.posts`. No schema changes. * **Responsiveness:** Perfect parity between Desktop and Mobile. * **Accessibility:** High contrast for readability in dark mode. ## 5. Success Criteria * The UI feels "intentional" and "branded" for a digital diarist. * Motion adds to the experience without being distracting. * The reading experience is significantly improved. * Consistency across all sub-pages (About, Archive, etc.).
